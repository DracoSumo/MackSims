# Compliance Questions Template

Use this worksheet before answering Apple or Google policy questions. This is a planning aid, not legal advice.

## App Access

- Does the app require login?
- Which auth providers are used?
- Is a demo account required for review?
- Can reviewers access core features without private approval?
- Does the app include account deletion?

## Privacy And Data

- What data is collected?
- What data is linked to the user?
- What data is shared with third parties?
- Is any data used for tracking?
- Is data encrypted in transit?
- What is the retention period?
- How can users request deletion?
- Are analytics, crash reporting, ads, maps, weather, payments, auth, or AI providers used?

## Content And Conduct

- Does the app contain user-generated content?
- Is there reporting, blocking, moderation, or admin review?
- Does the app include messaging or chat?
- Does it include public profiles?
- Does it include marketplace, reviews, ratings, bids, or offers?

## Permissions

- Does the app use location?
- Does it use background location?
- Does it use camera or photos?
- Does it use contacts?
- Does it use microphone, Bluetooth, calendar, notifications, or health data?
- Are permission prompts accurate and specific?

## Commerce And Regulated Areas

- Does the app include payments, subscriptions, tips, bookings, dispatch, brokerage, or escrow?
- Does it provide financial information, investment signals, or stock intelligence?
- Does it provide health, nutrition, training, medical, legal, safety, emergency, or transportation claims?
- Are disclaimers visible and accurate?
- Are any third-party partnerships claimed?

## Minors And Sensitive Audiences

- Is the app intended for minors or youth sports teams?
- Are parental consent or school/team policies relevant?
- Is any sensitive personal information collected?
- Are public profiles or messaging available to minors?
