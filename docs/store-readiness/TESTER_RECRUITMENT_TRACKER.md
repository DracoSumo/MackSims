# Tester Recruitment Tracker

Use this to manage public beta recruiting without mixing app feedback streams.

| Group | Target Count | Apps | Focus | Status |
| --- | ---: | --- | --- | --- |
| Friends and trusted early testers | 10-25 | ShutterBid, FishCrew | Install, navigate, report blockers | Ready to invite |
| Photographers and clients | 10-20 | ShutterBid | Role flows, profile, feed, marketplace copy | Needs list |
| Fishing community testers | 10-20 | FishCrew | Browse-first flow, Port Window, account switching | Needs list |
| Mobility/taxi reviewers | 5-10 | FairShare | Copy and planning review only | Later |
| Motorcycle riders | 5-10 | MotoCrew | v0.1 shell review only | Later |

## Invite Requirements

- Tester email.
- Device type and OS.
- App assignment.
- Build source: TestFlight, Play closed testing, or live web.
- Feedback channel and deadline.

## Feedback Process

- Route all public beta issues through support@macksims.com until a dedicated tracker is chosen.
- Ask for screenshots, recordings, device, OS, app version/build, and severity.
- Separate product ideas from launch blockers.
- Prioritize account state, blank screens, navigation failures, privacy/deletion gaps, and store-review blockers.
