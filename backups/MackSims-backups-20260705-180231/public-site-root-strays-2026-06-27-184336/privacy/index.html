Privacy Policy Draft

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

Scope

This draft covers MackSims beta apps and related services, including ShutterBid, FishCrew, FairShare, MotoCrew, pilot apps, public beta pages, support intake, and tester feedback.

Information we may collect

Depending on the app and test build, MackSims may process account information, profile data, uploaded content, app preferences, support messages, diagnostic details, device and OS details, and tester feedback. Some beta screens may use mock or demo data.

Permissions and device data

Future app versions may request permissions such as media access, notifications, camera, or location. GPS, background location, Bluetooth, calling, intercom, payments, and safety systems should not be treated as production-ready unless a specific build and notice says they are active.

How information is used

We use information to operate beta tests, troubleshoot issues, improve app flows, maintain account/profile behavior, review uploaded content, communicate with testers, and prepare store readiness materials.

Sharing

We may use service providers for hosting, app distribution, diagnostics, authentication, analytics, and support. MackSims does not sell tester personal information in this beta policy draft.

Deletion and support

Users can request account deletion or privacy help by emailing support@macksims.com. Some records may be retained where needed for security, legal, audit, abuse-prevention, or operational reasons.
