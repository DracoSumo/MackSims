Beta disclaimer

Test the apps, but do not rely on them for critical decisions.

Important beta notice

MackSims beta apps may contain mock, demo, preview, placeholder, or incomplete content. They are provided for testing product flows, mobile layouts, app-store readiness, support paths, and feedback.

Do not use beta apps for emergency, financial, legal, medical, navigation, or safety-critical decisions. GPS, payments, background location, live moderation, marketplace verification, intercom, Bluetooth, and safety modules should be considered planned or test-only unless a build explicitly states they are connected.

Test focus

Navigation, account state, profile ownership, responsive layout, copy clarity, and feedback flow.

Report issues

Send device, OS, app version/build, screenshots, severity, and reproduction steps to support@macksims.com.

Use test accounts

External testers should avoid uploading sensitive personal content unless needed for a specific beta test.
