Support

Get help with a MackSims beta app.

Email support@macksims.com with the details below.

Include this in your message

  • App name: ShutterBid, FishCrew, FairShare, MotoCrew, or another MackSims beta app.
  • Device and OS version.
  • App version, build, or test source such as TestFlight, Play closed testing, or web.
  • What happened, what you expected, and the steps to reproduce it.
  • Screenshots or screen recordings when safe to share.
  • Severity: blocker, high, medium, or low.

Account or profile issue

Tell us which provider or account type you used and whether old profile, crew, feed, or role data appeared after logout or account switching.

Emergency note

MackSims support is not an emergency service. Do not use beta apps or support email for urgent safety, medical, legal, navigation, or financial decisions.
