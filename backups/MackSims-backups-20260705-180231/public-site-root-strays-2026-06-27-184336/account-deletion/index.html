Account deletion

Request deletion of your MackSims beta account.

Email support@macksims.com from the account address if possible.

What to send

Include the app name, email or sign-in provider, username/display name, device, and any details that help us locate the account. Mention if the request is for ShutterBid, FishCrew, FairShare, MotoCrew, or another beta app.

What may be deleted

Account records, profile data, preferences, uploaded content, tester feedback, and app-specific data may be deleted or anonymized where technically and legally possible.

What may be retained

MackSims may retain limited records for security, abuse prevention, legal compliance, audit logs, backup recovery windows, support history, or dispute prevention.

Timing

We will review deletion requests as part of beta operations and may ask for extra verification before removing or anonymizing account data.
