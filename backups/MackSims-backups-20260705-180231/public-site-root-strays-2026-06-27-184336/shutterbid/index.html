ShutterBid

Beta marketplace foundation for visual work.

ShutterBid is being prepared for clients, photographers, and business or venue partners to test role-aware profiles, feed surfaces, portfolio paths, and early bidding/marketplace structure.

Tester focus

Verify that client, photographer, business, and venue flows render correctly on mobile and desktop. Focus on feed stability, profile navigation, role copy, public readiness pages, and support paths.

Known limitations

Real marketplace verification, production payments, dispute handling, live moderation, contracts, escrow, and final booking enforcement are not production-ready in the beta foundation.
