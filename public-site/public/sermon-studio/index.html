Sermon Studio

Sermon planning, scheduling, and ministry workflow support for pastors and church teams running weekly services.

External beta Churches & ministry teams

What is it?

Sermon Studio is a MackSims external beta for church and ministry teams. It focuses on sermon preparation, service scheduling, and the weekly workflow pastors and staff juggle between drafts, revisions, and delivery.

Who is it for?

Pastors, worship leaders, and ministry staff at churches that need structured planning without enterprise church-management bloat.

What problem does it solve?

Sermon prep lives in docs, notes apps, and shared drives that were never designed for ministry rhythm. Sermon Studio gives teams one focused workspace for planning and scheduling.

Current status

Sermon Studio is in external beta. Open the live app, try the core flows, and send feedback. Native TestFlight and Google Play closed testing use the shared external tester list — start at the beta testing hub.
