Aegis Intel

Your research command center — track tickers you choose, watch SEC filings, and review market intelligence in one mobile-first workspace.

Early access Research & market data

What is it?

Aegis Intel is a MackSims progressive web app for personal research workflows. Build a watchlist, get filing and market alerts, review dossiers, and keep notes on tickers you care about — without pretending to be a broker or advisor.

Who is it for?

Self-directed researchers, operators tracking public companies, and MackSims team members who want a focused watchlist and alert surface — not a bloated terminal or social feed.

What problem does it solve?

Research signals scatter across tabs, inboxes, and half-connected tools. Aegis Intel pulls watchlist tracking, SEC filing awareness, elevated-risk flags, and review tasks into one calm command center on phone or desktop.

Current status

Aegis Intel is in early access on the web. Open the live app, try the core flows, and send feedback. Native invites use the shared external tester list — start at the beta testing hub.

Test now

Web app: sprightly-lily-160925.netlify.app. Feedback: feedback@macksims.com.

Important disclaimer

Aegis Intel provides research support only — not financial, legal, tax, or investment advice. It is not a broker-dealer and does not execute trades. Do not use it for emergency or safety-critical decisions.
