ShutterBid

A photographer–client marketplace for jobs, bids, bookings, and profiles — built for shooters, clients, and venue partners.

External beta Photographers & clients

What is it?

ShutterBid is a MackSims external beta marketplace for clients, photographers, and business or venue partners. It supports photography jobs, profiles, bidding workflows, and partner participation.

Who is it for?

Working photographers, clients who need shoots booked, and venues or businesses that want structured job and bid flows instead of endless DMs.

What problem does it solve?

Photo work gets lost in texts, emails, and unstructured quote requests. ShutterBid gives both sides a clear marketplace structure for posting jobs, reviewing bids, and building profiles.

Current status

ShutterBid is in external beta — one of MackSims’ primary tester apps. Open the live demo, exercise client and photographer flows, and send feedback. Native TestFlight and Google Play closed testing use the shared external tester list — start at the beta testing hub.
