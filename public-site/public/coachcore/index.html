CoachCore

All-sports coaching and team accountability. No more guessing who is locked in.

External beta Coaches & athletic teams

What is it?

CoachCore is a MackSims external beta for coaches and athletic teams across sports. It focuses on accountability, readiness, and team coordination — giving coaches a clear read on who is prepared and who is not.

Who is it for?

Head coaches, assistant coaches, and team staff at youth, club, and competitive levels who need accountability without another bloated team-management platform.

What problem does it solve?

Coaches waste time chasing status updates — who watched film, who completed assignments, who is actually ready. CoachCore makes team readiness visible so coaches can coach instead of guess.

Current status

CoachCore is in external beta. Open the live app, try the core flows, and send feedback. Native TestFlight and Google Play closed testing use the shared external tester list — start at the beta testing hub.
