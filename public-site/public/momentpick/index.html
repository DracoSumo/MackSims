MomentPick

Your camera roll, sorted — AI tells you what to post, what to portfolio, and what to skip.

In build Everyday creators

What is it?

MomentPick is a MackSims mobile app that imports photos and videos from your phone, analyzes thumbnails with AI, and suggests whether each item is best for social posting, a professional portfolio, or skipping. Original files stay on your device — only compressed thumbnails are uploaded for analysis.

Who is it for?

Everyday people with great phone footage — travel, events, hobbies, family moments — who want help deciding what to share without scrolling through thousands of files.

What problem does it solve?

Camera rolls fill up fast. Most shots never get posted or curated. MomentPick gives you a triage workflow: import, get AI suggestions in plain language, swipe to decide, and build shareable portfolio collections.

Current status

MomentPick is in active build. MVP focuses on camera roll import, AI triage (social vs. portfolio vs. skip), and public portfolio links. Viral edit templates are planned for a follow-on release.

Privacy

Thumbnails only in MVP — not full-resolution originals unless you explicitly choose to upload later. Your media is not used to train AI models. See the privacy policy for MackSims data practices.
