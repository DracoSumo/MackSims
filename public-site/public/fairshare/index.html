CurbCue

Rideshare and local transport fare comparison with crowd-meter and surge-awareness — so riders can make smarter calls before they book.

External beta Riders & local transport

What is it?

CurbCue is the MackSims ride-compare beta for rideshare, taxi, and local transport options. It is designed around fare clarity, crowd conditions, and surge-awareness — not another generic ride-hailing clone.

Who is it for?

Riders, commuters, and local operators who want a clearer picture of transport options and pricing before committing to a trip.

What problem does it solve?

Transport pricing shifts fast — surge, demand spikes, and platform differences make it hard to know if you are getting a fair deal. CurbCue surfaces comparison and context so riders decide with better information.

Current status

CurbCue is in external beta. Open the live demo, test on mobile, and send feedback. Native TestFlight and Google Play closed testing use the shared external tester list — start at the beta testing hub.
