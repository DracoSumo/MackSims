FishCrew

A fishing community and water-tools app for anglers, charters, and crews who plan local days on the water together.

External beta Anglers & fishing crews

What is it?

FishCrew is a MackSims external beta built around fishing discovery, community activity, crew coordination, and practical planning tools for local water days. It is designed for browse-first access with areas for exploration, tools, guides, profiles, and port-window-style planning.

Who is it for?

Recreational anglers, charter crews, fishing meetup organizers, and anyone who wants a community hub for local water activity — not a generic social feed.

What problem does it solve?

Fishing communities scatter plans across group chats, weather tabs, and half-connected apps. FishCrew pulls discovery, crew coordination, and day-of planning into one focused workflow.

Current status

FishCrew is in external beta — one of MackSims’ primary tester apps. Open the live app, try the core flows, and send feedback. Native TestFlight and Google Play closed testing use the shared external tester list — start at the beta testing hub.
