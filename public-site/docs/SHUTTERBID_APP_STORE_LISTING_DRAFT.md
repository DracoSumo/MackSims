# ShutterBid App Store Listing Draft

## App Name
ShutterBid

## Short Description
Photography marketplace beta foundation for clients, photographers, and business or venue partners.

## Long Description
ShutterBid is a MackSims beta app foundation designed to help clients, photographers, and business or venue partners explore a photography marketplace workflow.

The beta may include account flows, job posting concepts, photographer profile concepts, bidding or booking foundations, and admin or review surfaces.

ShutterBid is still in beta. Features may change, preview data may appear, and some marketplace functionality may not be fully wired.

## Known Limitations
- Beta software
- No guaranteed bookings
- No claim of verified professionals unless verified and documented
- No background checks unless wired and documented
- No real payments unless payment provider is wired and verified
- Moderation/admin tools may be limited during beta
