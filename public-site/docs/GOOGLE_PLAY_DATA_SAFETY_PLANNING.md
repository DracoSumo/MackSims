# Google Play Data Safety Planning

Final Google Play Data Safety and Apple App Privacy answers must match the actual production build.

Potential data categories may include:
- Account info
- Profile info
- User-submitted content
- Uploaded images if enabled
- App activity
- Diagnostics
- Optional permission-based location only if wired and permissioned

Do not claim live GPS, live weather, push notifications, real payments, verified professionals, background checks, or live moderation unless implemented and verified.
