## Instagram / Snap stories

**How to post today**
1. Open https://www.macksims.com/beta/story.html on your phone.
2. Screenshot Frame 1 (text + QR). Optional: screenshot Frame 2 (QR-first) as a second slide.
3. Post to Instagram Story and/or Snapchat Story.
4. If you have link stickers: use `https://www.macksims.com/beta/`
